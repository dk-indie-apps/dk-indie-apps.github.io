# Privacy Policy

**Knight Fight**
**Effective Date:** March 8, 2026

---

## Overview

Knight Fight is a mobile game developed as an independent family project. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect (very little) and how we use it.

**In short: We do not collect any personal data. Everything stays on your device.**

---

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's standard storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us:

- **Game preferences** — Music, sound effects, and haptics toggle settings
- **High scores** — Your personal best score for each mini-game
- **Tutorial progress** — Whether you have seen the tutorial for each game

This data is stored only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers (advertising ID, vendor ID, or any unique identifiers)
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Photos, camera, microphone, or any sensor data
- Browsing history or search queries

---

## In-App Purchases

The app offers optional donations (tips) through Apple's App Store. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple using their secure payment infrastructure. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any transaction details beyond what Apple provides to developers in aggregate sales reports.

For information about how Apple handles your purchase data, please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

---

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks beyond Apple's own native frameworks (SpriteKit, GameplayKit, StoreKit, AVFoundation). There are no:

- Analytics services (no Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting services
- Social media integrations
- Tracking or attribution tools

---

## Children's Privacy

Knight Fight is a family-friendly game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. Since the app collects no personal data whatsoever, it is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

---

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data to share.

---

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard mechanisms, it is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

---

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your locally stored game preferences and high scores can be reset by uninstalling the app.
