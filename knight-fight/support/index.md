## Frequently asked questions

### My high scores disappeared

High scores and settings are stored locally on your device. If you uninstall and reinstall the app, they reset. The app does not sync to iCloud.

### I want a refund for my tip

In-app purchase refunds are handled entirely by Apple. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) and request a refund directly. We have no access to your payment information and cannot issue refunds ourselves.

### The app crashed

We'd love to fix it. Please email us with:

- Your iPhone model
- Your iOS version
- Which mini-game you were playing
- What happened just before the crash

### How do I disable music or sound effects?

Settings screen (gear icon on the main menu) — toggle music, sound effects, or haptics independently.
